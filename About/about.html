Our company, CitySprout, was founded in early 2023 with a steadfast commitment to supporting SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities. CitySprout is a vibrant urban gardening community website, and we have cultivated a thriving community of urban gardeners, passionate about transforming cityscapes into flourishing green havens. At CitySprout, we offer a dynamic platform for gardening enthusiasts to connect, share invaluable tips, and access a rich database of resources tailored to the unique challenges of gardening in urban environments. With a focus on fostering sustainability and green living, we are dedicated to empowering individuals to nurture their own green spaces, contributing to healthier, more vibrant cities for all!
